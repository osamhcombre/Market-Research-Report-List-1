<p><p>The Electric & Hybrid Vehicle Driveline Market encompasses various configurations: </p><p>1. **Series Driveline**: Primarily driven by an electric motor, with an internal combustion engine (ICE) acting solely as a generator. </p><p>2. **Parallel Driveline**: Both the electric motor and ICE can drive the wheels simultaneously, enhancing efficiency. </p><p>3. **Power Split Driveline**: Combines both series and parallel systems, allowing seamless transitions between power sources. </p><p>4. **Electric Driveline**: Fully reliant on electric motors for propulsion. </p><p>5. **Others**: Includes emerging technologies or novel hybrid architectures not fitting standard classifications.</p></p>
