Piezo buzzers use piezoelectric materials to produce sound, known for their compact size and low power consumption, making them ideal for various applications. Electromagnetic buzzers use an electromagnetic coil to generate sound, offering higher sound output and robustness. The 'others' category encompasses specialized or less common types of buzzers, catering to niche applications in consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
