<p><strong>The CBCT Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Image Intensifier Detector</li><li>Flat-Panel Imager Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CBCT (Cone Beam Computed Tomography) systems utilize different detector types, primarily classified into Image Intensifier Detectors and Flat-Panel Imager Detectors. Image Intensifier Detectors convert X-rays into visible light, enhancing image quality for better resolution but are bulkier and less efficient in dynamic range. In contrast, Flat-Panel Imager Detectors use digital sensors to quickly capture images, offering superior resolution, reduced patient dose, and compact design. Both types are crucial in various medical and dental applications, impacting diagnostic accuracy and treatment planning.</p></p>
