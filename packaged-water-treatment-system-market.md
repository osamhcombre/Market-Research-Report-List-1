<p><ul><li>Extended Aeration</li><li>Moving Bed Biofilm Reactor (MBBR)</li><li>Reverse Osmosis (RO)</li><li>Membrane Bioreactor (MBR)</li><li>Sequential Batch Reactor (SBR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The packaged water treatment system market includes various technologies for efficient wastewater management. Extended Aeration enhances aerobic digestion, while Moving Bed Biofilm Reactor (MBBR) uses microorganisms on moving supports for biological treatment. Reverse Osmosis (RO) effectively removes contaminants through a semi-permeable membrane. Membrane Bioreactor (MBR) combines biological treatment and membrane filtration for high-quality effluent. Sequential Batch Reactor (SBR) offers a time-based operation for effective wastewater treatment in batches. Together, these systems cater to diverse water treatment needs across industries.</p></p>
