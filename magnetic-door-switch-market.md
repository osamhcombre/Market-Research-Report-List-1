<p><p>The magnetic door switch market primarily comprises two types: embedded switches and surface mount switches. Embedded switches are designed to be installed within the door frame or surrounding structure, providing a discreet solution that enhances aesthetics. In contrast, surface mount switches are attached to the door's exterior or frame, offering easier installation and maintenance. Both types serve to detect door movement and ensure security, finding applications in residential, commercial, and industrial sectors for automated control and safety systems.</p></p>
