<p><p>The Recombinant Human Serum Albumin market is primarily divided into two types: OsrHSA and ScrHSA. OsrHSA (Orientated recombinant human serum albumin) is engineered for optimal stability and efficacy in therapeutic applications, often used in drug formulations and as a stabilizing agent. ScrHSA (Stereospecific recombinant human serum albumin) is designed to selectively bind to certain therapeutic compounds, enhancing drug delivery and circulation time. Both types cater to the growing demand for safer and more effective therapeutic proteins in medical treatments.</p></p>
