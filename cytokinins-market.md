<p><p>Cytokinins are plant hormones that promote cell division and growth. The market can be categorized into two main types: adenine-type and phenylurea-type cytokinins. Adenine-type cytokinins, derived from adenine, are widely used to enhance plant growth and delay senescence. In contrast, phenylurea-type cytokinins, which contain a phenyl group and urea, are known for their potent activities and prolonged effects on plant development. Both types are utilized in agriculture, horticulture, and biotechnology for improved crop yields and quality.</p></p>
