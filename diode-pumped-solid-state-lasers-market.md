<p><p>The diode-pumped solid-state laser (DPSSL) market can be divided into two main types: pulse type and continuous type. Pulse type lasers generate short, high-energy bursts of light, making them suitable for applications like material processing and medical procedures. In contrast, continuous type lasers produce a steady beam of light, ideal for tasks such as laser engraving and telecommunications. Each type caters to specific industrial and scientific needs, driving demand in diverse sectors including manufacturing, healthcare, and defense.</p></p>
