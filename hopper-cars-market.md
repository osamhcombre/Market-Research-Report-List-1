<p><ul><li>Covered Hopper Cars</li><li>Open Hopper Cars</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hopper car market comprises two primary types: covered hopper cars and open hopper cars. Covered hopper cars are designed for transporting bulk materials that require protection from weather elements, such as grains, plastics, and chemicals. They feature a roof and are equipped with bins for easy loading and unloading. In contrast, open hopper cars are suitable for heavier bulk materials like coal and aggregates, allowing for easy unloading via gravity. Both types play crucial roles in freight transportation and logistics.</p></p>
