<p><p>The millable silicone rubber market is divided into two main types: medical grade and industrial grade. Medical grade silicone rubber is designed for healthcare applications, ensuring biocompatibility, purity, and resistance to sterilization processes, making it suitable for devices, implants, and prosthetics. In contrast, industrial grade silicone rubber is tailored for manufacturing processes, providing durability, heat resistance, and flexibility for use in automotive, electronics, and construction sectors. Each grade is formulated to meet specific regulatory and performance standards for its respective applications.</p></p>
