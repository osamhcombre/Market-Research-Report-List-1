Dual secondary transformers provide multiple output voltages, catering to different load requirements. Single primary transformers have one input voltage, suitable for straightforward applications, while single secondary transformers offer a single output voltage. Each type serves distinct needs in power distribution, ensuring efficiency and reliability across various electronic devices and systems.</p></p>
