<p><p>The Continuous Friction Tester market consists of two primary types: Portable Continuous Friction Testers and Benchtop Continuous Friction Testers. In contrast, Benchtop testers are used in laboratory settings for more controlled and detailed testing of friction properties, ensuring precise measurements and repeatable results. Both types cater to diverse applications in industries like automotive, aerospace, and materials science.</p></p>
