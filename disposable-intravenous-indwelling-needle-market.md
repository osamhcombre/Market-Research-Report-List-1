<p><p>The disposable intravenous indwelling needle market is categorized into two main types: open and closed indwelling needles. Open indwelling needles, often equipped with a blunt cannula, allow for easy access to the vascular system but may pose higher infection risks. Closed indwelling needles, on the other hand, feature a sealed design that minimizes exposure to contaminants, enhancing patient safety. Both types are essential for various medical procedures, ensuring efficient drug delivery and fluid administration while prioritizing patient care.</p></p>
