<p><ul><li>Oil-Cooled</li><li>Air-Cooled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial transformer market is primarily divided into oil-cooled and air-cooled types. Oil-cooled transformers use insulating oil for cooling and offer high efficiency and better heat dissipation, making them suitable for large industrial applications. Conversely, air-cooled transformers rely on ambient air for cooling, making them more compact and easier to install. They are typically used in smaller settings or where space is limited. Both types play crucial roles in electrical distribution and energy management in various industries.</p></p>
