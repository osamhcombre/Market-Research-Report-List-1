<p><p>The steel track switch market includes several types designed for various applications in railway systems. Single turnout allows trains to diverge onto a different track, enhancing flexibility. Equilateral turnout is a symmetrical switch enabling trains to move smoothly between two tracks, ideal for busy junctions. Three-way turnout provides access to three tracks from a single point, optimizing space at crossings. Other variants may include specialized designs for industrial or freight applications, catering to unique operational needs in rail transport.</p></p>
