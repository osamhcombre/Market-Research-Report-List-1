<p><p>The dispensing guns market comprises three main types: manual caulk guns, air caulk guns, and electric caulk guns. Manual caulk guns operate through a simple trigger mechanism, using manual force for dispensing materials, making them cost-effective and ideal for small projects. Air caulk guns utilize compressed air to dispense caulk or sealant, offering a faster and more uniform application suited for larger jobs. Electric caulk guns provide precision and ease of use with automated dispensing, often preferred for professional applications requiring consistent output.</p></p>
