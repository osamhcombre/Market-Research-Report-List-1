<p><p>The sleep apnea solutions market includes key types of devices designed to manage obstructive sleep apnea. Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) machines deliver a steady airflow to keep airways open during sleep. Bi-Level Positive Airway Pressure (BiPAP) devices provide varying pressures for inhalation and exhalation, catering to patients with specific respiratory needs. Automatic Airway Pressure (APAP) machines adjust pressure levels dynamically based on real-time analysis of airflow, offering a customized treatment experience. Each type addresses different patient requirements for effective apnea management.</p></p>
