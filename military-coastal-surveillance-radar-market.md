<p><p>The military coastal surveillance radar market comprises different types, primarily X-Band and S-Band radar systems. X-Band radars are known for their high-resolution imaging and precision in detecting small targets, making them ideal for close-range operations and tracking smaller vessels in coastal areas. In contrast, S-Band radars offer longer-range detection capabilities and better performance in adverse weather conditions, useful for monitoring larger maritime areas. Together, these radar types enhance situational awareness and security along coastlines.</p></p>
