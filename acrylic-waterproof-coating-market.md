<p><p>The acrylic waterproof coating market is primarily divided into two types: liquid and dry formulations. Liquid coatings are applied directly to surfaces, providing an immediate waterproof barrier that is flexible and easy to use. They are ideal for intricate surfaces and quick applications. Dry coatings, on the other hand, are typically in powder form and require mixing with a liquid before application. Once applied, they form a durable, waterproof layer ideal for heavy-duty protection in various environments.</p></p>
