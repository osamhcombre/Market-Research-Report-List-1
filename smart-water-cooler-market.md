<p><p>The Smart Water Cooler market consists of two primary types: Bottled Water Dispenser (BWC) and Point-of-Use (POU) systems. BWC utilizes large water bottles that are replaced periodically, providing an easy solution for hydration in homes and offices. In contrast, POU systems connect directly to a water supply, offering a constant source of purified water without the need for heavy bottles. Both types incorporate smart technology for enhanced user experience, such as monitoring water usage and filtration status.</p></p>
