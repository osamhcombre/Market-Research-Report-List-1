<p><strong>The Mechanical Absolute Encoders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Singleturn Encoder</li><li>Multiturn Encoder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical absolute encoders market comprises two primary types: singleturn and multiturn encoders. Singleturn encoders provide position information within a single rotation, typically measuring up to 360 degrees, making them suitable for applications with limited rotational movement. In contrast, multiturn encoders track multiple revolutions, capturing positions over several rotations and offering a more comprehensive range for complex applications. Both types are essential in automation and robotics, enabling precise position feedback in various industries, such as manufacturing and aerospace.</p></p>
