<p><p>The IP Security Cameras market is divided into two main types: centralized and decentralized IP cameras. Centralized IP cameras operate through a central server that manages video storage, processing, and access, providing streamlined monitoring and control. In contrast, decentralized IP cameras are equipped with onboard storage and processing capabilities, allowing them to operate independently, reducing reliance on a central system. This distinction influences installation complexity, system scalability, and overall infrastructure requirements, catering to diverse security needs and preferences.</p></p>
