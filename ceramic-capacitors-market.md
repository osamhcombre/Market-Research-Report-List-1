<p><p>The ceramic capacitors market comprises several types, each serving distinct applications. Multilayer Ceramic Chip Capacitors (MLCC) are widely used in compact electronics due to their small size and high capacitance. Ceramic Disc Capacitors are typically found in high-voltage applications due to their robustness. Feedthrough Ceramic Capacitors offer reliable performance in power supply systems by allowing signals to pass through while blocking noise. Ceramic Power Capacitors are designed for high voltage and current applications, essential for energy storage and filtering in industrial settings.</p></p>
