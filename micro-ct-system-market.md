<p><strong>The Micro CT System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In Vivo</li><li>Ex Vivo</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Micro CT System market is categorized into In Vivo and Ex Vivo types. In Vivo Micro CT involves imaging live subjects, allowing for the assessment of biological structures and disease progression in real-time without invasive procedures. Conversely, Ex Vivo Micro CT focuses on imaging tissues or organs that have been removed from the organism, providing detailed insights into their morphology and composition. Both types play crucial roles in advanced biomedical research, drug development, and clinical diagnostics.</p></p>
