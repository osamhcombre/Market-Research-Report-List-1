<p><p>The Veterinary ICU Cages market can be categorized into two main types: Monoblock and Parallel types. Monoblock cages are designed as single, integrated units that provide enhanced sterilization and easier cleaning processes, making them suitable for critical care. In contrast, Parallel type cages consist of multiple compartments arranged side by side, allowing for efficient use of space and flexible configurations for various animal sizes. Both types prioritize patient safety and comfort while facilitating ease of monitoring and treatment.</p></p>
