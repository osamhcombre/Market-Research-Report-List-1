<p><p>Container tracking and security encompass technologies like Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) and Real Time Locating Systems (RTLS) to monitor and secure cargo. RFID uses electromagnetic fields to automatically identify and track tags attached to containers, providing accurate location data and preventing theft. RTLS offers real-time tracking by utilizing wireless networks, enabling precise monitoring of container movements within facilities. Both technologies enhance operational efficiency, improve supply chain visibility, and ensure the safety of goods throughout transportation processes.</p></p>
