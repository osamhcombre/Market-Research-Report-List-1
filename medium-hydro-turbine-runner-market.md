<p><p>The medium hydro turbine runner market encompasses different types, including Francis, Kaplan, Pelton, and others. Francis turbines are versatile for various heads and flow rates, ideal for medium to large plants. Kaplan turbines excel in low-head, high-flow applications, utilizing adjustable blades for efficiency. Pelton turbines operate in high-head conditions, using impulse force from water jets. Other types may include crossflow and screw turbines, catering to specific site requirements. Each type contributes uniquely to harnessing hydro energy effectively.</p></p>
