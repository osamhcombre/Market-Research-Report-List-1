<p><ul><li>Independent Type</li><li>Not Independent Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vehicle HVAC market comprises two main types: Independent and Not Independent systems. Independent HVAC systems function autonomously, allowing individual control of temperature and airflow in different vehicle zones, enhancing passenger comfort. In contrast, Not Independent systems rely on a shared central unit, controlling climate through the main system without personalization for specific areas. This type is often more cost-effective but offers less flexibility. Both systems cater to varying consumer preferences, impacting overall vehicle design and functionality.</p></p>
