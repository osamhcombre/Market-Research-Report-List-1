<p><p>Smartphone microscopes can be categorized into single tube and multi tube types. Single tube microscopes feature a single optical pathway, offering a straightforward design ideal for basic observations and portability. Multi tube microscopes, on the other hand, incorporate multiple optical pathways, allowing for enhanced versatility and advanced imaging capabilities. This design enables users to switch between different magnifications and focus levels, making it suitable for more detailed examinations and scientific applications. Both types cater to varying user needs in the smartphone microscope market.</p></p>
