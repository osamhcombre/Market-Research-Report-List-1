<p><strong>The Electric Vehicle Swap Station Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sub-box Replacement</li><li>Chassis Swap</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric Vehicle (EV) swap stations facilitate the rapid exchange of depleted batteries for fully charged ones. The market can be divided into two primary types: sub-box replacement and chassis swap. Sub-box replacement involves swapping only the smaller battery modules, allowing for quicker exchanges and lower costs. In contrast, chassis swap involves replacing the entire vehicle chassis, which includes the battery and drivetrain, offering a more comprehensive solution for long-range EV operation but at a higher complexity and investment.</p></p>
