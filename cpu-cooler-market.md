<p><p>The CPU cooler market primarily comprises air coolers and water coolers. Air coolers utilize heatsinks and fans to dissipate heat, offering a straightforward, cost-effective cooling solution with easy installation and maintenance. In contrast, water coolers employ a liquid cooling system that circulates coolant to efficiently transfer heat away from the CPU, often providing superior thermal performance and quieter operation. Both types cater to different user needs, balancing factors like performance, noise levels, and budget considerations in the market.</p></p>
