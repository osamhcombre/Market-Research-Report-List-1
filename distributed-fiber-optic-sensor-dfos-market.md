<p><p>Distributed Fiber Optic Sensors (DFOS) leverage fiber optic technology to deliver real-time measurements over long distances. The primary market types include Distributed Temperature Sensing (DTS), which monitors temperature variations, and Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS), which captures acoustic signals along the fiber's length. Other types, such as Distributed Strain Sensing (DSS), measure strain and deformation. These sensors find applications in industries like oil and gas, infrastructure monitoring, and environmental sensing, providing valuable data for safety and efficiency.</p></p>
