<p><p>The Medium Voltage Circuit Breaker market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Air Circuit Breaker (ACB)**: Utilizes air as the arc extinguishing medium, suitable for low maintenance and high reliability.</p><p>2. **Vacuum Circuit Breaker (VCB)**: Operates in a vacuum, providing superior arc extinction and compact design, ideal for industrial applications.</p><p>3. **Oil Circuit Breaker (OCB)**: Uses oil for insulation and arc quenching, commonly found in outdoor settings.</p><p>4. **SF6 Circuit Breaker**: Employs sulfur hexafluoride gas to effectively interrupt current, known for its excellent insulating properties and compact size.</p></p>
