<p><strong>The Sous Vide Cooking Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Immersion Types</li><li>Water Bath Types</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sous vide cooking machine market is primarily divided into immersion and water bath types. Immersion sous vide machines feature a heating element and a pump that circulates water, allowing precise temperature control for cooking in sealed bags. Water bath types, on the other hand, consist of larger containers filled with water, maintaining a consistent temperature without the need for additional equipment. Both types ensure evenly cooked food with enhanced flavors and textures, appealing to home cooks and professional chefs alike.</p></p>
