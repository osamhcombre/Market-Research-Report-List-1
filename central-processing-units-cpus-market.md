In the CPU market, BGA (Ball Grid Array) and CSP (Chip Scale Package) are key packaging types. BGA uses a grid of solder balls for mounting, providing better thermal performance and electrical connectivity, while CSP offers a compact design with dimensions close to the die size, optimizing space and integration in devices. Both types cater to varying performance needs and form factor requirements in the tech industry.</p></p>
