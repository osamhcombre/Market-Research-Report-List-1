<p><p>The industrial blades market includes various manufacturing techniques that enhance performance and durability. High-frequency welding utilizes electromagnetic energy to join materials, ensuring a robust seam for blades in high-demand applications. Laser welding employs focused beams of light for precise and quick bonding, allowing for intricate designs and minimal heat distortion.
