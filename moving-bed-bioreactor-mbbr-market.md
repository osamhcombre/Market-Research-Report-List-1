<p><p>The Moving Bed Bioreactor (MBBR) market includes various types based on oxygen conditions. Anoxic MBBRs support nitrification and denitrification processes, primarily used for nutrient removal. Anaerobic MBBRs treat wastewater without oxygen, promoting digestion of organic materials, ideal for biogas production. Aerobic MBBRs utilize oxygen to facilitate the breakdown of organic matter, widely adopted in municipal and industrial applications. Other market segments may involve specialized systems tailored to specific treatment needs, showcasing the versatility of MBBR technology.</p></p>
