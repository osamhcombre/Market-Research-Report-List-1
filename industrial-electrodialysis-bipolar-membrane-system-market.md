<p><p>The Industrial Electrodialysis Bipolar Membrane System market comprises Continuous and Batch Electrodialysis Systems. Continuous Electrodialysis Systems operate continuously, facilitating the ongoing separation and purification of ions, making them ideal for large-scale operations. In contrast, Batch Electrodialysis Systems process a fixed volume at a time, suitable for smaller applications or specific production needs. Both types utilize bipolar membranes to enable ion migration and assist in various separation processes, contributing to applications in water treatment, chemical manufacturing, and renewable energy.</p></p>
