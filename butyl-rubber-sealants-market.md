<p><p>The butyl rubber sealants market is categorized into two main types: vulcanized and non-vulcanized. Vulcanized type sealants undergo a curing process, enhancing their durability, resilience, and temperature resistance, making them suitable for demanding applications like construction and automotive uses. Non-vulcanized sealants, on the other hand, remain flexible and are easier to apply, providing effective sealing for various household and minor repair applications. Both types are valued for their weather resistance, adhesion properties, and overall performance in sealing solutions.</p></p>
