<p><p>Non-profit donation software exists primarily in two market types: on-premises and cloud-based. On-premises software is installed locally on the organization’s servers, offering greater control and customization but requiring ongoing maintenance and IT support. In contrast, cloud-based software is hosted online, providing easier access, automatic updates, and scalable features. This model typically reduces upfront costs, as organizations pay subscription fees, making it an appealing choice for non-profits seeking flexibility and accessibility in managing donations.</p></p>
